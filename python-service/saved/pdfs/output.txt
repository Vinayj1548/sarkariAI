Grade ‘C’ Stenographers Limited Departmental Competitive Examination, 2025 
Corrigendum 
F. No.4/2/2025-CONF-5: Candidates of Railway Board Secretariat Stenographers Service 
(RBSSS) and Central Vigilance Commission may please refer to the Notice of Grade ‘C’ 
Stenographers Limited Departmental Competitive Examination, 2025 published by the 
Commission on its website on 22.12.2025. 
2.        Consequent to intimation by Railway Board, Ministry of Railways regarding 
relaxation in eligibility criteria in respect of Stenographer-D of 2018 batch of RBSSS, the 
following insertion is made at the end of Para 1(a) “Length of Service” in the column 
“Conditions of Eligibility” of the table under Para 31 (Crucial date and Conditions of 
Eligibility) of Annexure-I of the Notice of Examination, in respect of RBSSS: 
Relaxation for Stenographer-D of 2018 batch of RBSSS, in terms of Rule 21 of 
RBSSS Rules, 2021: 
i. 
One year in Approved Service 
ii. 
Up to one year three months and five days in continuous service and 
iii. 
One year and five months in duration between date of result and crucial date of 
eligibility. 
3. 
Consequent to intimation by Central Vigilance Commission (CVC) regarding change 
in the “Crucial date for determining eligibility”, the following amendment is made in the 
column “Crucial date for Counting of approved service” in the table under Para 31 
(Crucial date and Conditions of Eligibility) of Annexure-I of the Notice of Examination, 
in respect of Central Vigilance Commission: 
For 
May be read as 
1st day of July, 2025 (01.07.2025) 
1st day of January, 2025 (01.01.2025) 
 
4.         The other terms and conditions of Notice of Grade ‘C’ Stenographers Limited 
Departmental Competitive Examination, 2025 shall remain unchanged.
